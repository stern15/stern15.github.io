Biography

Semasuka was born in Bujumbura,in Burundi in 1993 from a Rwandese dad and a Burundian mother,last born in a family of 4 children,Semasuka had an ordinary life,and was first exposed to technology at the age of 16..since that day he fell in love with computer and especially since he discovered the late discounted Encarta encyclopaedia software from Microsoft,he decided to focus all his effort in Geo-spacial data which was reenforced by his interest in geography..at the at age of 19,Semasuka joined the Eastern Africa university,Baraton based in Eldoret in the western of Kenya where he pursuit he ambition of becoming a geomatic engineer.After 3 year of study,he decided to join the prestigious MORINGA SCHOOL in Nairobi..he is still a junior developer but aspiring to become one of the brightest Geomatic engineer in Africa and therefore being an inspiration for many young Africa who think they don't have the potential because of limited resource of their continent.
